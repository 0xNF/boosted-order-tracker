About this app

Why?

We, the Boosted Board community, are very impatient and excited to receive our board. Some of us are constantly wondering if new shipments have been made

I have noticed a lot of posts on /r/boostedboards regarding order confirmation/status updates. This gave me the idea of creating this very simple app to let the community track Boosted's "order back log".

How does it work?

Users are authenticated anonymously. We use the browser session as a reference to identify you. This means that if you want to update the status of your order for others to see, you have to use the same computer and browser used for creating it. Otherwise, it will create a duplicate order status in the database. You can update the status of your order at all times. Just update the fields and hit save.

You never have to refresh the page. It works in real time as people are updating their order status on the app

Use it to reduce your BB anxiety ;). We are all very impatient to receive our Boosted Board
